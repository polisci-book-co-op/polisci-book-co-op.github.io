Welcome

{% include image.html url="/images/pip-rbook.jpg" caption="Pip has some books he'd like to share with you." width=275 align="right" %}

I've been in political science after I completed my undergraduate in the spring of 2006. Over those 12+ years, I've acquired several books along the way for either grad seminars or research. They make for a nice library but the truth is I don't need a lot of these books anymore. I want to give them to people who do need them.

That leads to this, for lack of a better term, "book co-op." Feel free to browse my collection. If you see anything you like, reach out to me via Twitter or via email and let me know what you want. I'll send them to you with only a polite nudge that you could maybe pay it forward by chipping in some money to my PayPal or maybe my Amazon account as a thanks or a means to help defray future shipping costs. You could also find me at a political science conference like MPSA and buy me a beer.

Don't worry about it if you're a cash-strapped student; find another way to pay it forward down the road.
